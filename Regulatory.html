Regulatory & Compliance

Navigating Zambia's Statutory and Regulatory Environment

Our department offers essential services to ensure your business adheres to all Zambian statutory requirements, minimizing legal risks and ensuring smooth operations with key regulatory bodies.

Business Registration & Statutory Filing

Company and NGO Registration

  • **Company Registration with PACRA** (Patents and Companies Registration Agency).
  • **NGO Registration** (Non-Governmental Organization) with relevant authorities.

PACRA Compliance and Documentation

  • **Annual Returns Filing, Computer Printouts** (to maintain good standing).
  • Intellectual Property (IP) Registration (Trademarks, Patents, etc.).
  • Processing amendments to company details (directors, address, share capital).

Statutory & Regulatory Liaison

We provide comprehensive support for all issues related to the following Zambian regulatory bodies:

  • **ZRA** (Zambia Revenue Authority) issues.
  • **Napsa** (National Pension Scheme Authority) related matters.
  • **NHIMA** (National Health Insurance Management Authority) compliance.
  • **ZPPA** (Zambia Public Procurement Authority) and public sector engagement.
  • **Workers Compensation** Fund Control Board (WCFCB) compliance.
  • **CEEC** (Citizens Economic Empowerment Commission) and **EIZ** (Engineering Institution of Zambia) related issues.
  • **Zamcops, Labour office, and Local councils** related issues.
