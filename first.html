Our Story & Vision

Company Overview & History

**PROPACT ENTERPRISE LIMITED** is a Zambian registered company, established on the 4th of June 2025. Founded on the vision to redefine the professional services landscape, we started as a specialized management consulting company.

We have since expanded significantly to offer a comprehensive recruitment division, HR provision, business registration, and support services covering PACRA, ZRA, NHIMA, and various compliance issues. Our aim is to provide **end-to-end solutions** for all our clients' talent, business, and regulatory needs.
